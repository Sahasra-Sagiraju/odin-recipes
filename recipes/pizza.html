Hummus Pizza Recipe

About Hummus Pizza:

Petya loves hummus and he loves pizza. So he decided to make Hummus Pizza. Instead of the usual red tomato sauce, he spread hummus on the pizza dough. He then added some broccoli, cherry tomatoes, mushrooms, olives and red onion. And... It turned out delicious.

Now, he wants to share his secret Hummus Pizza recipe with you!

Ingredients:

  • 1 (10 ounce) can refrigerated pizza crust dough
  • 1 cup hummus spread
  • 3/2 cups sliced bell peppers, any color
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 cups shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 475 degrees C (220 degrees C).
  2. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden brown and cheese is melted in the center, 10 to 15 minutes. Slice and serve.
