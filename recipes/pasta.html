Mexican Pasta Recipe

About Pasta

Petya's friend (whose name is not known), made some pasta for them to eat. He then boasted that nobody can make better pasta then him. Petya the chef, didn't like his arrogance and decided to prove him wrong. So, he went home and started experimenting in his kitchen. He tried for many years but failed to come up with a better recipe. He was sad...

So, he decided to go on a trip to his favorite place on the planet, Mexico. On his visit there, he observed many street food vendors. And one day, something clicked in his mind. He rushed home and made the most delicious pasta ever. He named it Mexican Pasta.

Now, he wants you to try his Mexican Pasta Recipe!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 pound seashell pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1/2 cup sweet corn kernels
  • 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can peeled and diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 3/2 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. While pasta is cooking, heat olive oil over medium heat in a large skillet. Cook onions and bell pepper in oil until lightly browned, about 10 minutes.
  3. Stir in corn and heat through. Stir in black beans, tomatoes, salsa, olives, seasoning mix, and salt and pepper and cook until thoroughly heated, about 5 minutes.
  4. Toss sauce with cooked pasta and serve.
